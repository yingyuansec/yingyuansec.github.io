I am a Postdoctoral researcher at Sapienza University of Rome, under the supervision of [Prof. Luigi Vincenzo Mancini](https://sites.google.com/di.uniroma1.it/mancini). My research interests include **Adversarial Machine Learning, AI Security, Web Security** and **HCI**, where I primarily focus on using Machine Learning for web security purposes and analysing the security of Machine Learning/AI systems. 


Before that, I was a Postdoctoral researcher at University of Padua (Italy, 2024-2025), where I was working on Privacy-perserving anti-malware. Prior to that, I was a PhD student (in Computer Science) at University of Padua (2020-2024), in the [SPRITZ Security and Privacy Research Group](https://spritz.math.unipd.it/index.html), supervised by [Prof. Mauro Conti](https://www.math.unipd.it/~conti/) and [Prof. Giovanni Apruzzese](https://www.giovanniapruzzese.com/). I was also a visiting scholar at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), supervised by [Prof. Gang Wang](https://gangw.cs.illinois.edu/index.html) (2023); and a visiting scholar at University of Liechtenstein (2023), supervised by Prof. Giovanni Apruzzese. The title of my PhD dissertation is "Machine learning for phishing website detection".

Before moving to Italy, I was a master student at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) (China, 2017-2020), where I was honored as the Outstanding Graduate of Beijing and Outstanding Graduate of BUPT. My master thesis focused on data integrity verification.
